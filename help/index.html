Be a Donut.js Organizer!

A lot work goes into Donut.js, and if you’d like to help make it happen, we’d love to hear from you.

We need help with things like:

  • Speaker outreach
  • Sponsor outreach
  • Setup and teardown at the venue
  • Check-in at the venue
  • Website updates
  • Posting to Twitter and Instagram before, during, after the event
  • Ordering and picking up donuts and LaCroix
  • Writing and sending emails
  • Posting event to Calagator, Facebook, Upcoming.org, etc.
  • Strategizing growth and direction for Donut.js
  • Coming up with new ideas and opportunities
  • …and more!

Sound good?

Email us at hello@donutjs.club with a few details about yourself and how you’d like to help.

Thank you!

Need a Conversation Topic?

Find someone who needs a conversation partner and ask them:

  • What’s your favorite kind of donut? What’s the best donut you ever had?
  • What’s something you’re passionate about that you’d give a talk on?
  • Would you most like to pet a capybara, a red panda, a quokka, a dog, or a cat?
  • Have you been to Donut.js before? What was your favorite Donut.js talk?
  • What’s a personal project you’d love to start or work on more?
  • How did you hear about Donut.js?
  • Do you have any favorite Twitter bots?
  • Which talks tonight are you particularly looking forward to?
  • Do you have some a favorite place in Portland you like to take visitors?
  • What’s your favorite animal?
  • What was your first username online?
  • What’s something you saw on the internet recently that made you feel joy?
